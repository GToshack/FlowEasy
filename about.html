Our story

After getting stuck in yet another country, lost luggage, dream hotel fully booked and no activity in sight, we decided this needed to change. It then became the mission of fixing this for the average traveler. We partnered with everyone and everything to make travel the most convenient, From deals with Frontier Airlines, shuttle buses, and hotels across the globe all the way to word of mouth with locals. After years of building up connections and word of mouth we are proud to give you a taste of connection — The FlowEasy way.

Who we are

We are a Canada based travel company, home-grown here in Ontario. We love our roots, and we give back to our community by hosting and sponsoring events around the province.
